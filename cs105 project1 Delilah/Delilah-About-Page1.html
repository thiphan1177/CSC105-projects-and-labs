About Us

This is Ella and Janet. We are sisters, pastry chefs and the founders of Delilah's, a French-American cafe named after our mother, Delilah Matthews. We opened this cafe in 1995 to bring to the beautiful town of Northampton, Massachusetts a taste of something sweet. As pastry chefs and coffee connoisseurs, our main focuses are on coffee and desserts. However, we do make several delicious soups and sandwiches as well - secret recipes passed down to us from our chef parents. We invite you to come and try them! They're all very yummy. Since our opening, we have expanded to include six more cafes located in Boston, Toronto, Montreal, Philadelphia, New York City and San Francisco.

In addition, we want to thank our customers for your never-ending support. Without you, there would be no Delilah's.

Our manifesto

We believe that delicious food is good for the soul. That's why we're constantly trying out ideas in the kitchen. Through trial and error, that's when the good stuff happens. From passion for our craft, we hope to bring a big dose of happiness to our customers.
