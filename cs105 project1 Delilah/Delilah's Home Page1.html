Well, hello there!

This is Ella and Janet and welcome to Delilah's, a warm and cozy cafe located in lovely Northampton, Massachusetts with several other locations spanning the United States and Canada. Along with our team of chefs, we handmake our artisanal desserts, savory dishes and coffees fresh everyday with locally (when possible) procured ingredients. Many of the recipes in our menu were passed down from our parents who were renowed culinary chefs. Many more recipes we developed ourselves after our time at culinary school in New York City and from our experiences working in France and Canada.
