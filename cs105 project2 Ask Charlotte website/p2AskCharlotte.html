Hello there!

I'm Charlotte Phan and welcome to my official website. If you don't know me, here is a little introduction. I am a professionally trained psychologist from the University of California at Berkley, reporter and advice columnist for San Francisco's newspaper, The Bay Mirror, and author of four best-selling books called Finding Love When You Are Busy Saving the World, How to Cherish Your Family and Friends and Not Forget, Saving and Investing Money the Cool Way, and The Untold Truths About Work That's Meaningful and Pays Well. I have a fifth book coming out that's called Bite Me: A True-ish Story About A Girl and A Vampire who Juggles. The main purpose of this site is so that you can personally email me about any advice you need. I try to always respond, but if you don't get a reply from me soon, I'm very sorry! Understand that I get a lot of emails and I can't read them all at once, although I wish I could.

How to use this site

Home: Contains introduction, info about me and how-to-use website instructions.

Write to me: Fill out the form and write what advice you would like me to answer.

Quizzes: Take a quiz that tests to see how much you know about being in a healthy relationship! Or take a quiz that results in you getting some good wisdom from yours truly after answering and selecting from a given set of questions and choices.

Other: This tab contains a dropdown menu of 3 pages which are about links to my favorite advice articles from other writers, my books, and an interactive javascript element.
